Hema

Bringing to you the story of Hema Rawat.

The right quality education, having sheer confidence in oneself, and, a soft nudge, can do wonders in bringing the best in one's personality.

Hema, an excellent student of MM Education Foundation, joined us in 2016, in Pratishthan Learning Centre. Having successfully graduated with flying colours in 2021, she reminisces her journey.

Developing an interest in English through the encouragement by her teachers, helped her manifold.

From a student, to now a successful professional working in Quickmetrix, we bring to you the story of Hema Rawat.

Saneema

Saneema, one of our Alumni, visited the school recently during the Annual Alumni Meet. She has been with MM Education Foundation since 2015.

In the beginning, she was studying at grades lower than her age, but through the Fast-Track education & skilling program, she is now working in a MNC.

We will be with her long-term & we are sure that over a 50 year period, she will make an extraordinarily beautiful transformationin her life, her family, her community & the country.

Rahat

Rahat took a leap of faith in 2014 & joined Lotus Petal Foundation. His journey began at the Pratishthan Learning Centre, a Fast-Track Learning Program, which helped him cover his fractured education.

Alongside his studies, MM Education Foundation helped him pursue a vocational course, which further helped him get a job. It has now been 4 years of his working successfully in the IT department of MM Education Foundation. Rahat recalls giving his first salary to his parents. The smile that he witnesses on their faces, spoke volumes of how happy they were.

Inspired by the journey & dedication of our Founder & Managing Trustee, Mr. Kushal Chakravorty, he wants to keep himself associated with MM Education Foundation & bring a difference in the lives of the students. Rahat has dedicated himself to giving back to the society & feels immense fulfilment in his role as a Changemaker at MM Education Foundation.
